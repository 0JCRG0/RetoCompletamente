Often, companies use an automated Applicant Tracking System (ATS) instead of having a human reading through CVs. For this reason, is highly important that CVs are ATS friendly. This involves clean formatting, standard file types, relevant keywords, prioritized content, quantified achievements, common fonts, error-free text and customization for each application.

We don't want you to take on this time-consuming task yourself, which is why in Your Job Tracking Hub, all you need to do is click one button to receive an optimized CV for each suitable job.

You'll need to give your approval for each new batch of jobs we upload to Your Job Tracking Hub. We are not going to apply to any jobs or use any optimized CVs without your consent.

For each RH Token® you purchase, we guarantee a minimum of 5 suitable jobs and an ATS optimized version of your CV for each job.
